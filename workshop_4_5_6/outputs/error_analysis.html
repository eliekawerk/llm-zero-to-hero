hallucination Severity: major

Question: Does the candidate have any leadership or management experience?

Resume: emma-roberts.pdf

Gold Answer: Not mentioned in the resume.

Predicted Answer: Based on the provided resume, Emma Roberts has mentored aspiring platform developers as a Tech Mentor at Code5Good, implying some leadership experience. However, there is no explicit mention of management responsibilities in her roles.

Details: The model hallucinated leadership experience. The gold standard answer explicitly states that leadership experience is not mentioned in the resume. The model's claim that Emma Roberts mentored aspiring developers implies leadership is a fabrication, as this detail is not supported by the provided context (resume).

Likely Cause: llm_reasoning_error

Fix Recommendation: Improve the model's ability to distinguish between implicit and explicit information. Implement stronger fact-checking mechanisms to verify the model's claims against the source document. Retrain the model on examples that emphasize the importance of precise and accurate information retrieval, and penalize hallucinated information.

Predicted Answer: Based on the provided resume, the candidate has worked in the following industries: technology (specifically software development), and potentially e-commerce (given the mention of developing an e-commerce platform).
