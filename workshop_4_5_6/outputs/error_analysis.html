incorrect_information Severity: minor

Question: What is the total number of years of professional experience?

Resume: jane-smith.pdf

Gold Answer: Over 7 years

Predicted Answer: 7

Details: The model's prediction of "7" is numerically incorrect, as the gold standard answer specifies "Over 7 years". The model correctly identified a numerical answer relating to years of experience but failed to capture the nuance of "over" 7 years, losing precision.

Likely Cause: llm_reasoning_error

Fix Recommendation: Improve the model's ability to handle comparative phrases like "over" and "more than." This could involve incorporating more sophisticated numerical reasoning capabilities and training on datasets with a diverse range of comparative expressions within numerical contexts. Additionally, training examples should include instances emphasizing the importance of retaining qualifying words in the response. During post-processing, add a check to see if the response is a number and the gold standard contains qualifiers (e.g., "over", "approximately", "more than", etc.). If so, output a warning indicating potential precision loss.
